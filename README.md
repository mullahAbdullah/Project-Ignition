Project Ignition — AI Lead Qualification Assistant

A production-ready AI Lead Qualification Assistant built with Streamlit and OpenRouter.

The assistant engages website visitors, qualifies leads through natural conversations, automatically extracts CRM information, scores lead quality, and helps businesses convert more visitors into customers.

---

## Features

✅ Natural AI conversations
✅ AI-powered lead qualification
✅ Intelligent lead scoring
✅ CRM profile extraction
✅ AI Sales Coach
✅ Multiple business modes
✅ Proposal generation
✅ PDF export
✅ CSV export
✅ Professional business-oriented closing flow
✅ Responsive dashboard
